Services

Exemplary for Top Quantity and Performance

We are a leading industry in providing multinational, multidisciplinary, and multi-functional organization. Over the past years of Danem’s operations, it has expanded region-wide existence in the whole Middle East region and Arabian Gulf. Danem has marked its status in the U.A.E, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Kuwait and Sultanate of Oman. Danem choice of services covers a variety of industries such as Power, Oil & Gas, Fertilizers, Steel & Aluminum, Chemical, Smelters, Field Erection Services, and Cement.

Danem's major activities include:

Fabrication / Assembly

  • Pressure vessels, tanks, silos, stacks, ducts, chutes
  • Boiler pressure and non-pressure parts
  • Industrial steel structures, pipe racks, conveyor structures, platforms
  • Ship loader, ship unloader and stacker reclaimer
  • Piping - CS, Alloy and SS
  • GT exhaust and air intake systems
  • Gantry, semi gantry, overhead cranes

Fabrication / Assembly

We are a leading construction enterprise (EPC) offering in-house services in:

  • Design and fabrication,
  • On-site installation,
  • Inspection,
  • Testing and maintenance.

Field Erection

  • Static, rotating equipments
  • Pipelines and pipings
  • Conveyor systems
  • GT exhaust and air intake systems
  • Industrial steel structures, pipe racks
  • Boilers, allied equipments and structures
  • Material handling equipment - ship loader, unloader, stacker reclaimer, conveying systems

Field Services

  • Annual turnarounds and shutdowns
  • Surface preparation and coating
  • Allied civil, electrical, instrumentation works
  • Skilled manpower and equipment support
  • Desludging and rehabilitation of storage tanks
  • Ultra jetting cleaning services
  • Scaffolding

Offshore Services

  • Provision of work boat, support vessels
  • Repair/ maintenance of offshore drilling rigs, production stations, well heads
  • Replacement/ modifications piping

  • Skilled manpower, equipment and spare parts support

All the activities listed above are accomplished through our various Business Units /Group Companies located at Qatar, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and Ras Al Khaimah.
